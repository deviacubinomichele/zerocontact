PRIVACY POLICY

1. Introduction

ZERO CONTACT ("we", "our", or "us") is a mobile game developed by Michele Iacubino. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our game.

2. Information We Collect

ZERO CONTACT is designed as a completely offline game. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information or data from your device to any server.

Specifically:

  • We do NOT collect personal identification information
  • We do NOT collect location data
  • We do NOT collect device identifiers
  • We do NOT use analytics or tracking services
  • We do NOT use advertising SDKs
  • We do NOT have any server infrastructure

3. Local Data Storage

The game stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Game progress (completed levels, scores, ratings)
  • User preferences (theme, language, audio settings, vibration)
  • Avatar (2 initials and a color, chosen by the user)
  • Unlocked uniforms and abilities
  • Friend leaderboard entries (pasted manually by the user from shared codes)
  • Ghost replay data (stored in IndexedDB)

This data is stored using your device's local storage (localStorage and IndexedDB) and is never transmitted to any external server.

This data can be deleted by:

  • Using the in-game "Reset Progress" feature (Settings menu)
  • Uninstalling the game
  • Clearing the app's data in your device settings

4. Sharing Features

The game allows users to manually generate and share text-based codes containing their game score, rank, and avatar initials. This sharing is entirely user-initiated (via the device's share dialog or clipboard) and does not involve any server or automatic transmission.

5. Third-Party Services

ZERO CONTACT does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or libraries that collect user data. There are no ads, no analytics, and no tracking.

6. Children's Privacy

ZERO CONTACT does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.

7. Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • VIBRATE: To provide haptic feedback during gameplay
  • WAKE_LOCK: To prevent the screen from turning off during active gameplay

These permissions are used solely for gameplay functionality and do not involve any data collection or transmission.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: dev.iacubino.michele@gmail.com
